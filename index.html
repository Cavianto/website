What should I expect after the service is paid for?

After the service has been paid, here are the steps we follow:

1. We will send you an invitation to join our Trello board for project management. This board will keep you updated on your project and act as our official communication channel.

2. We’ll send you a questionnaire to gather the information you want included in your web form, such as files, colours, images, and other details.

3. If you’re already using a website builder, hosting, domain, or integration apps, we’ll request access from one of our team members at Cavianto for those services.

4. If you're unsure about which website builder, hosting, domain, or integration apps you need to establish your business, we’ll provide a list of recommended services tailored to your needs. We can assist you in purchasing and configuring these services, which will be paid for by you.

5. You’ll receive our initial service proposal, and you can request as many revisions as necessary.Once everything is finalized, we’ll deliver the completed project to you.

How can I access any of the four services offered by Cavianto: Social Media Design, Email Marketing, Web Design, and Website Maintenance

1. Visit the Pricing section on our website and choose the option you need by clicking the "Start Now" button.

2. You’ll be redirected to a new site where you can fill out the details and requirements for your business.

3. Next, you’ll be taken to the payment page; select your preferred payment method and complete your purchase.

4. Finally, you’ll receive an email with instructions to get started with your new service.

How can you assist me if I need to add or enhance features to my services that aren’t included in your plans?

If your project requires additional features, increased capacity, or specific needs, please reach out to us at hello@cavianto.com or give us a call +(61) 1300 505 870. You can also fill out a form, and we’ll provide you with a personalised quote and calendar for your project.
